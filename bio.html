Biography

Aydın I. Kuru is a Turkish researcher, composer and electronic musician based in London. Born and raised in Kadıköy, Istanbul, Kuru has communications, music and sonic arts degrees from Galatasaray University, Istanbul Technical University and the University of Sussex, respectively. Doctoral candidate of Music Technologies at the University of Sussex since 2025, Kuru’s research and academic practice focuses on generative music, psychoacoustics, multimedia audio and musical interfaces. In his artistic pursuits, Kuru’s performance and compositions are most heavily influenced by mixed composition and computer music and has been platformed in multiple academic conferences. Kuru’s electronic music practice also extends to a decade-long adventurism in Turkey’s underground scene which saw him perform in the majority of Istanbul’s club circuit in addition to illegal raves and art galleries.
