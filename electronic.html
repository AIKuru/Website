Electronic music

Aydın Kuru (aka Strahlung, Baba Ibra) operates as DJ and promoter since 2016 — most known for his performances in the now defunct legendary bass music club Pixie Underground and as the main resident and musical director of 6t6, an Istanbul-based promotions firm with 20+ international bookings across Istanbul, London, Berlin, and beyond. Kuru has shared the line-up with some of the strongest techno acts of his strain of dance music such as Perc, Ansome, Chris Liberator, Nico Moreno, Scalameriya, Manni Dee, Tommy Holohan, JKS, OGUZ and [KRTM] to name some.

For booking and enquiries: aydin@6t6.org
