Aydın I. Kuru

Aydın I. Kuru is a researcher, electronic music practitioner and a PhD student at the University of Sussex — his works sit at the intersection of art and technology: focusing on music for multimedia, acousmatic composition, live performance and live-coding.

Based in London, UK.

For contact: a.kuru@sussex.ac.uk.
