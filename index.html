Aydın I. Kuru

Aydın I. Kuru is a researcher, composer and Music Technology PhD student at the University of Sussex in Brighton, England. He has his background in Communication and Media Studies (BA), Music (MA) and Music and Sonic Media (MA), and his work focuses on music for multimedia, acousmatic composition, live performance and live-coding.
